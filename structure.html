What does the day-to-day reality look like for these programs? Explore the teaching styles, curricula, selection criteria, and more.

Praxis Program One-year funded fellowship with weekly meetings, expected joint working time, a single collaborative project, and public documentation throughout the process.
CHI Initiative One-year funded fellowship for individual project work; students meet weekly, participate in a field school session, and present work at conferences throughout the year.
HOPE Mellon Scholars Three-year project: two-semester interdisciplinary seminar; year-long, Oxford-style tutorial, including summer study; capstone project for public presentation.
CUNY DHI One-year renewable fellowship with an expectation of 15 hours of work per week; fellows meet weekly to gain skills and discuss projects.
UCLDH Three terms of study: first two are various modules of coursework, while the third is a thesis project and job placement.
