The Praxis Network is a suite of differently-inflected programs providing innovative methodological training and research support for humanities graduate students in order to equip them for scholarship and future careers.

If you're considering developing a similar program, or if you'd simply like to know more about existing developments, we invite you to explore the site and find out more about the successes, challenges, patterns, and unique aspects of these exciting programs.
