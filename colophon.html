Website Credits

This website was a 2013 collaboration between Katina Rogers, Senior Research Specialist at the Scholarly Communication Institute, and Jeremy Boggs, Design Architect for the Scholars' Lab at the University of Virginia Library, working with the guidance and design direction of Bethany Nowviskie of the University of Virginia and Association for Computers and the Humanities. Bethany, Jeremy, and Katina would like to thank Abby Smith Rumsey, Director of the Scholarly Communication Institute, for convening conversations at SCI that led to the formation of the Praxis Network.

Website 2014 additions, the Praxis Network Student directory and the Related Programs directory, were collaboratively designed and built by Purdom Lindblad, Head of Graduate Programs, Scott Bailey, Humanities Developer, Wayne Graham, Head of R&D, and Jeremy Boggs, with the guidance of Bethany Nowviskie.
