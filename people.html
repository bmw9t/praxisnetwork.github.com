--- layout: category title: People ---

In every case, the people involved make these programs what they are. From the directors that guide them to the graduate students that are the lifeblood, each program takes on distinct characteristics in large part due to the people involved.

Praxis Program An annual cohort of six students from a variety of disciplines, directed by Bethany Nowviskie, with collaboration, guidance, and instruction from the Scholars' Lab Research & Development team.
CHI Initiative A variable cohort of doctoral students from disciplines related to cultural heritage, under the direction of Ethan Watrall.
HOPE Mellon Scholars A highly selective undergraduate program directed by William Pannapacker, Mellon Scholars also involves faculty representatives from all departments in the Arts & Humanities division of the college.
CUNY DHI Directed by Matt Gold under the purview of the Provost's Office, the first cohort of digital fellows are doctoral students from a variety of backgrounds.
UCLDH This program is highly interdisciplinary; students enter with a range of backgrounds and participate in modules across the faculties. The program is directed by Claire Warwick and Melissa Terras.
